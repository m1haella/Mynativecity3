My native city is Chișinău, the capital of Moldova. Even though it is not one of the most famous cities in Europe, it has a unique atmosphere that makes people feel welcome. For me, Chișinău is more than just a capital city. It is the place where I grew up and created many memories.

№ 01 · Nature

A Surprisingly Green City

One thing that makes Chișinău special is the number of parks and green areas. Many people come here to relax, walk, or spend time with friends after a busy day.

Ștefan cel Mare Central Park · The heart of the city

One of the most beautiful places is Valea Morilor Park. It has a large lake, long stairs, and peaceful walking paths. Especially in the evening, the view there is amazing.

"Another popular park is Ștefan cel Mare Central Park, located right in the city center, often full of students, families, and tourists."

№ 03 · Daily Life

Everyday Life in Chișinău

Life in Chișinău is quite calm compared to many other capitals. People go to school, work, cafés, and markets, and the city has a relaxed rhythm that is hard to find elsewhere.

🌸SpringParks bloom. The city awakens.
☀️SummerStreets fill with life.
🍂AutumnGolden leaves, warm cafés.
❄️WinterA calm settles over the city.

During spring and summer, the city becomes more lively. Streets and parks are full of people enjoying the warm weather. Students especially like spending time in the center of the city, turning every bench and fountain into a gathering place.

Chișinău after dark · The city glows with a quiet warmth

№ 04 · Reflection

Why Chișinău Is Special to Me

What I like most about Chișinău is that it feels both like a capital city and a comfortable place to live. It is not too crowded, and many areas are quiet and peaceful, a rare quality for any capital in Europe.

It has its own charm that people can discover if they visit. Not a postcard city, not a tourist machine. Just a real, breathing place with real people and real stories.

✦ ✦ ✦

For me, Chișinău is not only a place on the map. It is home.
